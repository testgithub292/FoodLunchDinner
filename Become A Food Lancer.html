Who is a foodlancer?
You probably have heard about freelancer right! A foodlancer is self employed person who offers his food services to any company or individuals he/she wants to. All women who have a passion of cooking and want to make money from home via lunch.pk are referred as foodlancers.
How a woman can sell food online using lunch.pk?
The hardest part of any business is selling and getting customers. No matter what you do and wherever
you are if you have no sales you will be out of business soon. at lunch.pk we took this burden off your
shoulders. We provide our foodlancer women guaranteed customers near their location. you get
guaranteed sale/money into your pocket using our homemade food portal.
