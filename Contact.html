Got Questions? We Have Got Answers

Which Cities Are You Available?

We are available in 4 cities and expanding. Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad and Rawalpindi. We will be
available in more cities soon.

Are You Available In My Area or Street?

You can simply send us your location on WhatsApp at +92 333 5189005 and we'll share details and
menus for your area

What Makes Lunch.pk Different From Other Food Portals?

Almost 98% of the food apps and portals connect you with the restaurants and hotels near you. On the
other hand, Lunch.pk connects you with our verified foodie families living near you. We accept only
homemade food and kitchens backed by expert women chef well known for their quality food to give
you a real taste of homemade food and healthy meals at your doorstep. Moreover, we have quality
assurance processes and regular checks to make sure that you get clean, hygienic and healthy food via
home kitchens. We do not accept poor kitchen environments, bad taste or any hotels or restaurant
food.

What Is Minimum Order For Daily Deals?

We need order of minimum 5 persons for daily deals. However, if you are a single subscriber, you can
check out our monthly or weekly packages here.

What If I Want Food For 6 or 7 Days A Week?

If you want food for 6 days or 7 days a week, all you need is to pay extra amount for those additional
days and let us know while filling out the form.

How Much Food Is Served In A Meal Plan?

We offer 1 serving in our weekly monthly meal plan. the food is more than enough for 1 person but we
do not recommend 2 persons to use single meal plan. Each meal includes 1 dish, 2 chapati, salad or
raita. Our 5 serving deals are more than enough for 5 persons with drink, salad, raita and roti.

What Payments Methods Do You Accept?

All of our payments are advance. You can pay us on your first order via Bank Transfer or Cash on
Delivery

What If I Am Not Available?

You will receive 5 meals a week. If you are not available at your address for any reasons, just inform us
1 day ahead on our phone +92 333 5189005 Your days will be adjusted when you are back and
available.
